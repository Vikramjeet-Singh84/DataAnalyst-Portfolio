SQL & Power Bi Project

"Pizza Sales"

In this project, I need to analyze key indicators for the pizza sales data to gain insights into business performance. Specifically, I will calculate the following metrics:
1. Total Revenue
2. Average Order Value
3. Total Pizzas sold
4. Total Orders
5. Average Pizzas Per Order

Chart Requirements:
I will have to visualize various aspects of the pizza sales data to gain insights and understand key trends. And then I identified following requirements for creating charts:
1. Daily Trend for Total Orders
2. Monthly Trend for Total Orders
3. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Category
4. Percentage of Sales by Pizza Size
5. Total Pizzas Sold by Category
6. Top 5 Best Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity, and Total Orders
7. Top 5 Best Sellers by Revenue, Total Quantity, and Total Orders
